Main flow:
	Press Start to play Brain War game
	Choose the right answer by click button in 5 seconds.
	If you have a correct answer, you will got 100 points.
	If you have a wrong answer, you will lose 100 points.
	If you can't have an answer after 5 seconds, game over.
	After game over, press "Retry" to play again, press "Cancel" to exit game.
